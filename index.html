Welcome to the official page of the Dinagyang Festival, one of the most vibrant and culturally rich festivals in the Philippines. Held every January in Iloilo City, the festival celebrates faith, culture, and community through colorful street dancing, dramatic performances, and exciting competitions.

Inspired by the devotion to Santo Niño, Dinagyang showcases the creativity and energy of local tribes who perform choreographed dances in elaborate warrior costumes while telling stories from history and tradition.

Visitors from across the Philippines and around the world gather to experience the festival's electrifying atmosphere, delicious local food, and warm Ilonggo hospitality.

Experience the rhythm, culture, and spirit of Dinagyang!
